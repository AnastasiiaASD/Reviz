# Business Plan

## Overview

The Business plan is the main paid tier for property managers with larger portfolios or who need advanced features. It includes everything in Basic/Premium plus AI response generation, bulk imports, Owner Reviews, rich snippets, and customisable SEO pages. There are two pricing structures — **old pricing** (users registered before November 24, 2022) and **new pricing** (users registered on or after November 24, 2022). Prices diverge on tiers above 49 properties.

## Supported Currencies

EUR, USD, GBP. Rates are converted from EUR using ECB exchange rates.

---

## How Old vs New Pricing Is Determined

The system checks the following at subscription time:

1. If the user's account was created **before November 24, 2022** → old pricing product is used (`Revyoos Subscription 2021 tramos`).
2. If the user holds a **Premium plan** → always treated as an old-pricing user regardless of registration date.
3. All other users → new pricing product (`Revyoos Subscription 2022 tramos`).

Old-pricing users keep their rates when staying on the same tier. Moving to a higher property tier or changing billing interval applies the old pricing structure to the new tier.

---

## Upgrading & Downgrading

- **Mid-cycle upgrades** — prorated charges applied immediately.
- **Downgrade** — not allowed if the target tier has fewer property slots than the user's current active property count.
- **Basic / Premium → Business** — all Business features unlock immediately after payment.
- **Business → Business AI** — sentiment analytics and AI summaries unlock immediately.
- **Business AI → Business** — sentiment analytics and AI summaries are removed.

---

## Yearly-Only Features

The following are only available on yearly billing regardless of plan tier:

| Feature | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Data export to CSV / Excel | Business or Business AI, yearly billing |
| API connection for custom reports | Business or Business AI, yearly billing |

## Plan ID Format

```
{plan}_{interval}_{properties}_ppt
```

Examples:
- `business_monthly_49_ppt` → Business plan, monthly, up to 49 properties
- `business_yearly_99_ppt` → Business plan, yearly, up to 99 properties
- `businessAi_monthly_199_ppt` → Business AI plan, monthly, up to 199 properties
